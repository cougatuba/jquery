Welcome to Guess the Movie! where we find out how well you know your movies.

The game is simple, you will be able to select a range of years and will have to guess movies that were released during that time period.

You will then be shown a movie still from a random movie and will have to guess the title of the film. If you aren't sure what movie it is feel free to ask for a hint.

Hint #1 reveals the main three actors. Hint #2 reveals what roles they played. Hint #3 reveals the plot of the film.

If you still can't guess it after all three hints, feel free to skip to the next round! There are 5 rounds total and points are awarded for guessing the movie without using hints.
